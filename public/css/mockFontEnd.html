The Oregon Path

-You have a long journey ahead. The choices you make along the way will determine if you and your party make it.

-You start will 100 points

-Each question will have 3 choices.

-The best answer will deduct 0 points. The second best answer will deduct 2.5 points. The worst answer will deduct 5 points.

-You start will 5 party members including your self. You will lose a party member after -20 points.

-Good luck!
